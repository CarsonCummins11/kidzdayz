Long Weekend

Monday, December 10, 2012

We had a 3 day weekend this weekend.  Carson kept himself busy building an awesome new robot.  His last one was a robotic arm that could raise and lower things.  He thinks these things up and designs them all on his own.  He spends time sketching out ideas in his notebook and then just starts experimenting.  This is a little boy who really needs a parent who is an engineer and can help him with his creations!!  This one is much more sophisticated than others in the past and required multiple trips to Radio Shack where he bought diodes, switches and various other things.  I kept asking him "do you know what that is"?  He didn't appreciate me asking that in front of the Radio Shack employees who were helping him...  Here it is - it is designed to float around in the pool and attract anything metallic...
