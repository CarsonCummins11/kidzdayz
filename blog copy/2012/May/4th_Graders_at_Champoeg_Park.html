4th Graders at Champoeg Park

Friday, May 11, 2012

Carson and I went on the 4th grade field trip yesterday to Champoeg Park.  It was a beautiful day and the kids really liked the historical presentations about the life of the Kalapooya Indians.  I had a great time until the bus ride home when about 60 4th graders were screaming "We Will Rock You" at the top of their lungs lead by one of the teachers....
