NW Cross Country Challenge

Saturday, September 21, 2013

Today the kids both competed in the NW Cross Country Challenge down in Eugene.  It was on the same course where all the state XC meets are run (and were run 20+ years ago by their mother :-)).  They both did awesome!  Carson was 20th out of 76 6th-8th graders and Lucy was 70th out of 207 high school girls.  A very fun day!
