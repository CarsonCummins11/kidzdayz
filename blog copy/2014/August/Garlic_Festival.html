Garlic Festival

Saturday, August 9, 2014

With dad's lack of mobility we're somewhat limited in what we can do as a family at the moment.  So, getting creative, we decided to check out the Garlic Festival which takes place every year in a small town very near our house.  It was a blast and just what you might expect from a garlic festival ... complete with a Garlic Queen, Garlic Ice Cream, Lots of really good food, Music and dancing and the local senior citizen home selling homemade pie!  A very fun evening for us :)
