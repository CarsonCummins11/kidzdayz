The Best for Last

Tuesday, June 24, 2014

Our last stop on our tour was to visit Andy's sister and her family in Buffalo, NY.  We could not have had a warmer welcome or a better time!  Our first day was a very exclusive and all inclusive tour of greater Buffalo followed by a trip to the truly spectacular Niagara Falls!  That evening we celebrated Lucy's birthday along with her cousin Ben who turned 25 2 days after Lucy turned 15.  Such a fantastic day with family!
