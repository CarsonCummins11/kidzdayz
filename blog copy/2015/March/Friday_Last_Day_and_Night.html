Friday - Last Day and Night

Sunday, March 22, 2015

Friday may well have been my favorite day.  We rented an umbrella and two lounge chairs on the beach for the entire day and that's where I stayed reading my book alternating with swimming to cool off!  Lucy hung with me for a while and then went off to meet up with two friends from school who both happened to be staying at the same hotel as us.  Andy and Carson came for a while but then headed off to a sports bar to watch the NCAA tournament for a while and then to play soccer a bit (we never travel without a ball!).  That night our hotel had a fantastic fireworks display and then we went for a long walk along the beach!  Truly sensational!
