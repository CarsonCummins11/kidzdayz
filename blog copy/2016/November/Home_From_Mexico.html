Home From Mexico

Monday, November 14, 2016

Lucy's home from what sounds like a fabulous trip!  They had so many great experiences!  They learned a ton about the border and immigration, they hiked on a common trail people use to try to sneak into the US leaving food and water along the way, they worked with kids whose parents have been deported, toured factories and generally had a spectacular time.  We missed her and didn't necessarily love this precursor to her being gone on a more permanent basis next year!
