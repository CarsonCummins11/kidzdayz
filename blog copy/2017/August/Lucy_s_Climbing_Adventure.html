Lucy's Climbing Adventure

Sunday, August 6, 2017

And Lucy may have had the coolest adventure of all...  For the past 4+ years she's been an intern in a medical research lab at Oregon Health Sciences University.  Her mentor, Dr. Jan Boitz has played a huge role in Lucy's life and her role at OHSU certainly helped her college applications!  As one last (very big) hurrah, Jan invited Lucy to go climb the South Sister Mountain in Central Oregon.  They went with Jan's friend Ian.  They drove over early on Saturday morning, hiked to what's known as the "false summit" and set up camp for the night.  At 4am they hiked to the summit in the dark.  They were up there for a pretty sensational sunrise.  Lucy absolutely LOVED it!  And what a fabulous way for her to end her time with Jan.  We couldn't be more grateful for the relationship these two share!!
