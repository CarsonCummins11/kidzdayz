And Finally to Madrid

Monday, July 3, 2017

Last stop, a few days in Madrid - a very vibrant, historical and modern city.  We stayed in a fantastic apartment right near El Retiro park - similar to NYC's central park - so awesome!  Carson's favorite part of Madrid was not the royal palace or the amazing museums... no he was beyond thrilled because we found a Steak and Shake restaurant!  He couldn't have been more thrilled!  After 2.5 weeks in Spain we were ready to head home but were a little sad to leave behind such a magnificent country!
