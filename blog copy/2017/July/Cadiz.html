Cadiz

Friday, July 7, 2017

After Arcos it was time to move on to Seville.  On the way we decided to stop at one of Spain's many beach towns.  We didn't see this as a must-see for future visits but we did go to see a camera obscura which was pretty spectacular and had a little time on the beach.  Not a bad side-trip but not one we'd say is necessary to visit.
