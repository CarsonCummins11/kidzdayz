Ronda - The Gem of the White Hill Towns

Tuesday, July 11, 2017

After our hike - and on Lucy's 18th birthday - we drove to Ronda.  This may have been our favorite place of all!  It is one of the white hill towns.  Established in the 9th century it is one of Spains oldest towns.  It's built on the top of the  El Tajo gorge and is centered around two amazing bridges.  This town is also the birthplace of the "art" of bullfighting.  We visited the old town, did a waterfall hike/rappel trip, visited the bullfighting museum, had some fantastic food and enjoyed a lovely old hotel in a very tranquil setting.  Carson had to do one of his long runs here as well so managed to find a trail where he was able to run 12 miles with spectacular scenery!
