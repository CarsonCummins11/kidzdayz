Sierra Nevadas of Spain

Wednesday, July 12, 2017

We rented a car in Granada to start our adventures through the white hill towns of Southern Spain.  We decided to leave a little early and start off with a hike.  We found one online called the Hanging Bridges Hike.  We had some fits and starts trying to find the start of the trail and got a bit lost along the hike but it was a great hike and well worth the effort :)
