Toledo

Tuesday, July 4, 2017

Our next stop was Madrid.  We arrived in Madrid on Saturday evening and headed out on a day trip to the hill-top town of Toledo on Sunday morning.  It was once the capital of Spain before it was moved to Madrid.  The cathedral in Toledo is one of the best I've ever seen and the town itself was so charming.  Another lovely day in Spain.  We walked the narrow streets, toured some museums along with the cathedral, sampled the town's famous Mazapan dessert dish (same as marzapan we decided but yummy all the same!).  We took a taxi tour of the area with a driver who only spoke Spanish.  Lucy did a fabulous job of acting as our official translator (as she did throughout our 2.5 weeks in Spain!) So fun to watch her manage herself (and us) in another language and another country!
