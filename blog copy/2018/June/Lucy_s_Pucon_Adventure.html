Lucy's Pucon Adventure

Tuesday, June 26, 2018

Lucy just got back from a weekend trip to Pucon, Chile.  It's a beautiful ski town that's on a giant lake in the Andes.  A friend of a friend of mine happens to live there with her family so she and her two friends were invited over for dinner :).  They also visited hot springs, did a lot of hiking in the surrounding mountains, hung out on the beach and went kayaking.  From all accounts it was truly spectacular!!
