Beautiful Copenhagen

Wednesday, December 18, 2019

Copenhagen is such a fabulous city where everyone takes bikes everywhere!  We toured the royal palace which is right in the city, rode bikes all over the city, saw the famous mermaid statue, did a lot of walking/touring/shopping and generally had a lovely time!  It was so much fun to have Lucy as my tour guide showing me the city she'd been living in for the past 4 months. She truly loved living there!
