Leavenworth Weekend

Sunday, October 18, 2020

The first day was to Eight Mile Lake. There was a LOT of wind. We saw 4 big trees fall. It's SO loud - sounded like gunfire or fireworks. Luckily the driver was fine but the car was totaled! Definitely not a good day to be in the forest!
