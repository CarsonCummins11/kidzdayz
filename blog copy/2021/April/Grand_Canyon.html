Grand Canyon

Saturday, April 10, 2021

I left Sedona and, kind of spur of the moment, decided to do a hike in the Grand Canyon. I will say that it was a fantastic hike with stunning views. I will also say that they're not kidding when they say you shouldn't just keep hiking down because you've got time and you're not feeling tired at all... Coming back up is NO JOKE!! I made it out but it was as hard as most people would realize it is. Well worth doing, but next time I will go in a lot more prepared :)
