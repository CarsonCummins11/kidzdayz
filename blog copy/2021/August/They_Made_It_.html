They Made It!!

Saturday, August 7, 2021

Seven days and 100+ miles with SOOO much elevation gain and loss! They made it back. It sounds like it was exhausting, stunning, and challenging!  They had a really good time but aren't super anxious to go out again right away. They're still friends and aside from a lot of bug bites, some scrapes and bruises and one soon-to-be lost toenail, they made it back in tact. Now that they're back safely, I'm super proud of them and happy they did it :)
