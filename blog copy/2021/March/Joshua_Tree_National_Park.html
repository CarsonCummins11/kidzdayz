Joshua Tree National Park

Monday, March 29, 2021

After more than a year of pandemic isolation we decided we needed some outdoor fun in the sun! We headed down to Joshua Tree National Park in Souther California. We met up with friends who happened to be taking the same trip at the same time. Had the very best time with Scott and Katrina!! Joshua Tree is beautiful with it's massive rock formations, beautiful blue skies, cacti everywhere, and those Dr. Seuss trees! Lots of hiking, catching up and laughing. Exactly what we needed!
