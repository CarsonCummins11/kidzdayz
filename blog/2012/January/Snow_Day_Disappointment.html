Snow Day Disappointment

Tuesday, January 17, 2012

The kids went to bed last night with PJ's inside out and spoons under their pillows and woke up this morning to the snow they'd hoped for.  However... there was no school closure nor even a 2 hour delay.  I had a couple of VERY surly kids on my hands this morning.  Lucy did take a moment to go out and play around with Zoe for a few minutes before making her grumpy way to the bus stop...
