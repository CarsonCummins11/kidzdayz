Climbing in Red Rock

Thursday, March 28, 2013

Day 2 - Rock Climbing in Red Rock
We stayed about 15 miles away from the strip at a place called Red Rock Resort.  We picked this place because of the National Conservation Area near by which has some of the best outdoor rock climbing in the country if not the world.  We spent an entire day with a guide.  Mostly the kids climbed one route after another.  Toward the end the guide set them up to practice rappelling down on their own.  Andy did a few climbs and I was the official lounger and photographer.  It was a sensational day.  The kids LOVED it!!
