Fun With Friends in New Hampshire

Tuesday, July 1, 2014

From Maine we headed on to New Hampshire to visit our very good friends Beth and Dave Towle.  We could not have had a better time with them.  Our first day we went on the most epic hike any of us had ever been on, it took 8.5 hours and exhausted the adults among us.  It was straight up a boulder packed mountain where we crossed over multiple streams/rivers.  Many of them we had to take off our shoes and really scramble to make it across.  The kids were truly in heaven!!  Andy and I loved it but were very sore the next few days!
