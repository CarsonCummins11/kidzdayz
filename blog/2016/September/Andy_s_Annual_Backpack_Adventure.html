Andy's Annual Backpack Adventure

Saturday, September 3, 2016

Andy returned from his annual backpacking adventure.  This is their 6th year and, by all accounts, was just as fabulous as it always is.  They hiked around the Three Sisters Mountains in Central Oregon.  They spent 4 days, had some fantastic scenery, some mediocre food, and great company!  From the pictures it looks to me as if Andy was, true to form, asleep for a lot of it! :)
