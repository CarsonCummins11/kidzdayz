Tahoma Travel Meet

Monday, September 12, 2016

Carson was one of the three freshman boys who were chosen to go on the travel meet this year.  They had to leave at 4am for the Tahoma co-ed relays.  They had an all freshman team with 3 boys and 2 girls.  They did really well coming in 12th out of 40 teams.  It was apparently a REALLY challenging course which included a run through a lake.  They had a great time.  After the race they had a BBQ at the park and then went (with all 30 highschoolers) to The Great Wolf Lodge for the night.  It sounds like it was quite a fabulous weekend!!
