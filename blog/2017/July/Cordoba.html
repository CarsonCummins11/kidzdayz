Cordoba

Wednesday, July 5, 2017

After Seville we took the train to Cordoba.  I was thrilled that Lucy completely took over planning our time in Cordoba.  She did a fantastic job!  We toured the Mosque of Cordoba, the Alcazar, and the Roman Bridge.  We walked around the winding streets of the Jewish Quarter, did some shopping and had a walking tour where we learned about the town's history.  Another hit!  Many great things to see and do in Cordoba!
