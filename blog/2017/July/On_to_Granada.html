On to Granada

Thursday, July 13, 2017

On Wednesday we flew from Barcelona to Granada in Southern Spain.  The main reason one visits Granada is to see the amazing Alhambra, a palace and fortress built originally by the Moors in the mid 13th century and then taken over when the Christians conquered the region in 1492.  It is a huge complex that is well worth a visit!  While the Alhambra was spectacular, the city of Granada is worth a visit on it's own merits.  We stayed in the old town and were captivated by the narrow cobbled streets surrounded by all white homes and businesses. We truly loved Granada!  The south of Spain is truly magical :)
