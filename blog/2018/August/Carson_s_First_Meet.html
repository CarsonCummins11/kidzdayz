Carson's First Meet

Saturday, August 25, 2018

Carson't first meet of the year is always the Wilsonville Night Meet - 3K.  They race by school year.  The Junior race was crazy fast and super crowded!  In the beginning of the race someone fell right in front of Carson - I watched him hurdle the guy and barely stay upright!  Crazy race!  He had a great race and came in 10th out of 150.  His team all came in the top 25.  It's going to be an exciting year!!
