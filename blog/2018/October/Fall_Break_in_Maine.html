Fall Break in Maine

Wednesday, October 17, 2018

This year Lucy is living in a living-learning community called Sophomore Scholars in Residence - Global Health.  It has been a fantastic experience so far and this past weekend they went on a trip together for their fall break.  They flew into Boston, played there for the day and had dinner with alums who are doing cool jobs in public health.  After, they drove up to Acadia National Park in Maine.  They packed a lot into 4 days!  They visited and toured several labs, hiked, went sea kayaking and climbed Cadillac Mountain at night to sleep out and be the first in the US to see the sun rise :)  From all accounts it was an excellent time for all!
