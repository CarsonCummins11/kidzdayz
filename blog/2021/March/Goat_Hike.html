Goat Hike

Sunday, March 28, 2021

While we were down in Joshua Tree, Katrina found a "Goat Hike". We signed up and drove more than 5 miles down a dirt road to meet up with a man who had moved from Paris and is now living in the middle of the desert in So. Cal. with about 30 goats (which he inherited when he bought the property). He taught himself to care for the goats and eventually started up a tourist business to allow people like us to go for a long hike in a beautiful desert, rocky area at sunset with about 20 goats :). Before starting the hike we got to hang out in pens with all the babies :) :) :) As much as it sounded a bit odd, this was a real highlight of the trip!! I mean, hiking and goats?! What could be better?!?!
