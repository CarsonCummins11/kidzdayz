Western States - California Dreamin'

Sunday, June 26, 2022

Carson has followed distance running since he was in middle school. His ultimate dream is to run the Western States 100. He and Andy rented a car and drove from SF to Lake Tahoe. They camped out and got up at 4am for the 5am start. After the start, they did a hike and then drove to one of the aid stations before driving on to the finish line before driving all the way back to San Francisco. I can't imagine running 100 miles through the mountains, heat and wilderness of Northern CA but if/when Carson decides to do it, we'll definitely be there on the support crew :)
