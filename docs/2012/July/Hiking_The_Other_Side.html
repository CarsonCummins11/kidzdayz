Hiking The Other Side

Sunday, July 8, 2012

The weather cooperated again today!  We headed up the opposite side of the valley from where we've been spending all our time.  We took a train up to a resort village called Wengen, then a gondola up to a much higher destination - Mannchlein.  From there we hiked to a place called Klein Schleig.  It was truly stunning - yet again!
