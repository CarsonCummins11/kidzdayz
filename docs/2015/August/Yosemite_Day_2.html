Yosemite - Day 2

Sunday, August 23, 2015

Our second day was our epic-hike day!  We started at beautiful Glacier Point and hiked first to Illilouette Falls, then on to Nevada Falls, Vernal Falls and then back down to Yosemite Village.  It was a whole day of hard hiking, beautiful scenery, swimming and sliding.  Truly an unforgettable day!
