Middle School Mile - Track Town USA

Friday, May 8, 2015

For the second year, Carson got to participate in the Middle School Mile which is held at U of O's Hayward Field - aka, Track Town USA!  It's in the middle of a giant regular meet which features Olympians and World Champions from all over.  They do the first half of the regular meet then the Middle School Mile and then the second half of the meet.  Such a cool event!  Last year Carson ran it in 5:48.  This year he was down to 5:26.  That's a pretty fast mile for a 7th grader!!
