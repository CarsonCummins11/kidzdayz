Montserrat

Saturday, July 15, 2017

We arrived later in the evening in Barcelona but decided to go ahead and do our day trip to Montserrat on Sunday.  Montserrat is a beautiful Benedictine monk mountain retreat.  It was cool to see the monastery but we really loved doing a big hike high above the monastery.  The kids went off on their own and managed to get somewhat lost on the way back down.  Luckily they were able to figure it out and make it back to us :)  Such a great day.  Everyone was pretty tired on the way back to Barcelona on the train!
