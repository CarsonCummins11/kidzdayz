Great Weekend in Sisters with Good Friends

Sunday, September 19, 2021

We have a group of buddies we've been taking trips with since our kids played kick and chase soccer together in kindergarten. No kids or dogs this time but we still had a great time! We went to Sisters, OR and did some cycling and hiking along with lots of eating, drinking and chatting.
