Maybe a Bit Much?

Sunday, May 29, 2022

Carson was very excited for me to bring my bike to SF. We rode from his apartment all through Golden Gate Park, along the beach, through the Presidio, across the Golden Gate Bridge, through Sausalito and Marin and then up to the top of Mt. Tamalpais. It was just an absolutely stunning ride! Truly probably one of my top 2-3 ever! I would strongly recommend that anyone do this ride! BUT.... it was SO hard! It was a bit over 60 miles and more than 10 miles uphill to get to the top of Mt. Tam. It had to be so painful for Carson to ride at my pace up the mountain but he stayed with me and kept me entertained and moving the whole time. Such a great trip! Thanks so much to Carson for being the best host ever!
